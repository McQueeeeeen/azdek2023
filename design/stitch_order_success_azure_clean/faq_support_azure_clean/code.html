Shipping & Global Logistics expand_more
We ship globally with carbon-neutral carriers. Standard shipping within North America typically arrives in 3-5 business days. International orders may take 7-14 business days depending on customs clearance in your specific region.
Sustainability & Refill Philosophy expand_more
Azure Clean is committed to circularity. All glass vessels are designed for permanent use. Our refill pouches use 85% less plastic than traditional bottles and are fully recyclable through our partnership with specialized recycling facilities.
Clinical Ingredient Standards expand_more
Our formulas are bio-based and non-toxic. We exclude sulfates, parabens, and synthetic fragrances. Detailed MSDS sheets are available for all products upon request for clinical transparency.
Real-time Order Tracking expand_more
Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a unique tracking ID. You can also view live progress within your member archive under 'Active Shipments'.
